# Suggested follow-up experiments

1. **Orthogonal validation of top induced genes**
   - Validate `IL36G`, `KRT16`, `IFI27`, `CLEC7A`, and `SPRR1A/B` by qPCR in an independent psoriasis cohort.
   - Confirm protein-level induction of `KRT16`, `IVL`, and `TGM1` by immunofluorescence or immunohistochemistry.

2. **Dissect immune-versus-epidermal components**
   - Perform cell-type deconvolution or integrate matched single-cell / spatial transcriptomic data to determine whether interferon and neutrophil programs arise from infiltrating immune cells, activated keratinocytes, or both.

3. **Treatment-response validation**
   - Reanalyze pre/post anti-IL17A or anti-TNF datasets to test whether the cell-cycle/interferon/cornified-envelope axis collapses in responders.

4. **Barrier-function assays**
   - Pair transcriptomic findings with transepidermal water loss or organotypic skin-culture assays to test whether the cornified-envelope signature tracks functional barrier disruption.

5. **Network-prior refinement**
   - Replace the generic STRING edge set with keratinocyte- or skin-specific interaction priors to improve biological specificity of the pathway-network view.
